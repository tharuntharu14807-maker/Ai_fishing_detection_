About AI Phishing Detector

Welcome to the AI-powered phishing detector, a project dedicated to enhancing online safety by identifying suspicious URLs quickly and accurately. Built using state-of-the-art machine learning techniques, this tool helps users protect themselves from phishing scams, one of the most common cybersecurity threats today.

The detector analyzes various URL characteristics and predicts whether a site is safe or potentially malicious, providing an easy-to-understand result with probability scores.

This project is continually evolving, aiming to incorporate more advanced AI models and broader datasets to improve detection accuracy and user experience.

Feel free to explore the project details or get in touch for more information or support.
